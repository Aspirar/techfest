Whizzing through the land of maths and science , this event will take you through the world of deciphers, while testing your basic fundamental maths and science skills every step of the way.

Problem Statement

Untangle the tangle is a quirky sudoko puzzle where the participants have to put a digit from 1 to N in each cell of the grid so that every row, column, bold region contains each digit once, where N is the number of rows and columns in the puzzle. But here is the catch; to unveil the first few numbers the participants are required to answer some of the technical questions which would lead them to a number. The numbers thus obtained needs to be added to the cells and consequently the sudoko has to be solved.

Rules

  • Remote participation is not allowed. Student have to be present at the venue.
  • This is an individual event.
  • Duration of the event is 90 minutes.
  • Participant who will solve the Sudoko in minimum time will win the event.
  • Students are required to come with their own laptop and charger.
  • Leaderboard will be displayed on the website after completion of event.
